About me

Horacio Manuli

Freelance Web Developer

I'm a Web Designer/Layout Developer. I make very beautiful pages, from the visual point of view, which is my task. I work freelance, and I charge for work done.

I consider myself a proactive person, incorporating new knowledge every day. Growing, through the studies of new programming languages, which I insert into the websites that I create.

I love freedom, independence. For that reason, I choose to work freelance. Agree with my employer, individually, on price, form of work, etc., and carry out that work remotely.

Obviously, I'd love to work as a team. I think that I'd learn a lot from it, in terms of web design, and how to function in an organization.

Look at my pages and contact me, so we can talk about that project you want to do so much.
